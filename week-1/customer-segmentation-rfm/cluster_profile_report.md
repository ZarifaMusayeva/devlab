# Cluster Profile Report — Customer Segmentation

We grouped 4,338 customers into 3 segments, based on how recently, how often, and how much they buy.

**VIP / Champions (770 customers).** These customers bought something only 17 days ago on average, they order often (about 13 times), and they spend the most money (£7,905 on average). They are the most valuable group. The business should keep them happy with loyalty rewards, early access to new products, or personal offers.

**Regular / Loyal (1,696 customers).** These customers buy sometimes (about every 44 days), order a few times (about 3-4 times), and spend a medium amount (£1,265 on average). This is the largest and most stable group. Regular email campaigns and small discounts can turn more of them into VIP customers.

**At Risk / Hibernating (1,872 customers).** These customers have not bought anything for a long time (about 167 days on average), they order rarely (about once), and they spend little (£363 on average). This group may leave the business soon. A win-back campaign, such as a discount code or a "we miss you" email, could bring some of them back.

Overall, the VIP group is small but very valuable, the Regular group is the backbone of the business, and the At Risk group needs attention before it is lost completely.
